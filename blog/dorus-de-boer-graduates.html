Dorus de Boer Successfully Defends His MSc Thesis

  • Tue 29 November 2022
  • news
  • Jason K. Moore
  • #bicycle, #engineering

Dorus de Boer successfully defended "Modeling a Professional Road Cycling Race To Determine the Fastest Wheel-Tire Combination". He developed a simulation based model that can assess past road races and test whether different wheels and tires would have improved the overall race time. This information can be used to inform choices about future races and provide quantitative metrics for these choices. Dorus was co-supervised by Harm Ubbens (Team DSM), Piet Rooijakkers (Team DSM), and Jason K. Moore (TU Delft). Everyone at the bicycle lab is very proud of Dorus and wishes him the best in his next step in his career.
