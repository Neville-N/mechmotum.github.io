First Lab Bicycle Tour!

  • Fri 09 June 2023
  • news
  • Jason K. Moore
  • #bicycle, #social

Several members of the Bicycle Laboratorium went on our first bicycle tour, which we hope to make an annual tradition! We bicycled from Delft to the Wortel-Kolonie in Belgium. Belgium hosts free camping spots "bivakzone" for through hikers and bikers around the country and we stayed at the one located in the Wortel-Kolonie.

Those that started in Delft did about 260 km over the two days and I did about 220 km due to the route passing through Rotterdam.

The approximate route we took from Delft to the Wortel-Kolonie and back.

Here we are at the beginning about to descend into the Maastunnel.
